Pilar Tarnera is the daughter of a family which was set their journey with José Arcadio Buendía, because her parents want her to separate from a man who raped her at the age of fourteen. He continued loving her, he never publicly acknowledged his relation with Pilar Tarnera. He promised to follow her "to the ends of the earth". But he never did and Pilar Tarnera lost her youth waiting for this man.

Relation with Buendías

Pilar Tarnera comes to the Buendía house for household chores. She later becomes the mistress of Jose Arcadio and Colonel Aureliano Buendía. Both of them have children with her, Jose Arcadio's son's name is Arcadio and Colonel Aureliano Buendía's son's name is Aureliano Jose. Aureliano José dies in war and, Arcadio continues the Buendia dynasty further.
