Strong and prudent matriarch of the house Buendia, Ursula keeps an eye on the house and manages it best to her capability. She doesn't seem to happy with the involvement of Jose Arcadio Buendia's scientific maneuvers. She tries to stop him. Ursula lives a long life, protecting and managing the gigantic Buendia household. Ursula never failed to support the family in any way and has a strong influence on almost every family member until she lived.
